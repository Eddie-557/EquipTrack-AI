# EquipTrack AI (Industrial Asset Management)

### The Vision
A data-driven solution designed to bridge the gap between heavy engineering infrastructure and modern AI. Developed during my tenure at **NASENI**, this tool automates equipment logging using Computer Vision (OCR).

### Key Features
- **AI-Powered Scanning:** Uses EasyOCR to extract data from analog gauges and equipment nameplates.
- **Predictive Maintenance:** Automates the calculation of service dates to prevent industrial downtime.
- **Built for Scale:** Backend developed in Python (Flask) with a React frontend.

### Why this matters
As an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Ibadan, I am focused on applying **Data Analytics** and **AI** to solve real-world engineering bottlenecks in the Nigerian energy and manufacturing sectors.
